The Hamilton Grange School

Spring 2014
Identity, Print

The Hamilton Grange School is a public middle school founded on the principle that hard works breeds talent, intelligence, and success. I was asked to create an optimistic identity for this new school in West Harlem, NY. Originally “Meliora,” the name underwent several changes during (and after) the design process.
