AgeAgo

Summer 2014
Identity, Print

AgeAgo is an app that leverages TMDb API to quickly find the age of your favorite actors when their films were first released. Film buffs can also test their knowledge with a fun guessing game before the age is revealed. I worked with theLIFT to capture the essence of the app in a clear brand guide. Sections include logo usage, color, typography, and word art. Below is a summary of the 25 page brand guide.
