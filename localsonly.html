Locals Only!

Winter 2015
Interactivity, Game, Fabrication

"Locals Only!" is a board game about surf localism. "Locals" and "kooks" carefully (or recklessly) navigate a crowded lineup in order to catch as many waves as possible. Shakas are awared for epic barrels, daring manuvers, and long rides. The surfer with the highest combined shaka score wins.

"Locals Only!" was featured in the 2015 UCLA Game Art Festival at the Hammer Museum. Designed for all audiences, it entertains both kooks and locals alike.

TOOLS:
Wood, Paper, Acrylic, Lasercutter, Plotter, Paint

RECOGNITION:
2015 UCLA Game Art Festival
UCLA Game Lab
